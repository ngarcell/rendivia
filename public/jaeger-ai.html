What We Do

From fragmented signals to coordinated enterprise action.

Instead of stopping at reports, the platform turns enterprise signals into structured context, applied intelligence, automated workflows, and live operational visibility. Platform Layers

A structured path from ingestion to operations.

The Jaeger AI platform is organized as a five-layer flow so data, logic, intelligence, and user-facing execution stay connected. Each layer has a specific job, and together they form the operating fabric for enterprise decision systems.

  1. 01

    Data Ingestion

    Connect batch, streaming, and system-of-record inputs across enterprise applications, data lakes, sensors, and third-party feeds.

  2. 02

    Data Modeling

    Normalize fragmented records into governed operating models that preserve business context, relationships, and lineage.

  3. 03

    Intelligence

    Apply analytics, predictive models, graph reasoning, and drift-aware ML services to generate decision-ready insight in real time.

  4. 04

    Agent Layer

    Orchestrate agentic workflows, escalations, approvals, and exception handling across multi-step operational processes.

  5. 05

    Operational Dashboards

    Deliver live dashboards, alert surfaces, and decision views that keep teams, operators, and leaders aligned on current conditions.
